About us

The Alentejano wines are produced in a region of Portugal called Alentejo. The soils mark the profile of our wines. Limestone soils are deeper and more fertile, originating grapes with greater balance and freshness, combining with schist soils, poor and austere lands, source of concentration and minerality. Each has its own expression, together they complement in every wine.

The estate located in the heart of Alentejo, which focuses its main activity on the production of wines with grape varieties with a high potential for adaptation to the soil and climate of the property, innovating in the productive details, which give character and savoir faire to its wines.

Its greatest ambition is the genuine preservation of this rural landscape and that its wines convey the best of the essence of its land, its vineyards and terroir.

An innovative project, where respect for nature and excellent protection of the local fauna and flora prevail, combined with the production of wines of unquestionable quality, with a cellar of unparalleled value and classic wines, which represent the best of the essence of their origin, protecting the land from an agricultural, visual and environmental point of view.
